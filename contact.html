Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What should I do if the game won't start?

A: Please ensure your browser supports JavaScript and has a stable network connection. Try clearing your browser cache or switching browsers.

A: Please email feedback@watervsfire.com with a detailed description of the issue and your browser information.
